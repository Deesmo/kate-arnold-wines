About Kate Arnold

Family · Farmers · Winemakers

Welcome to Kate Arnold Wines

Founded by Kate and Jean Arnold, our family-owned wine company is driven by a deep love for wine, an unwavering commitment to exceptional quality, and a genuine appreciation for the growers and distributors who make it all possible.

"When Kate and I first started Kate Arnold Wines, we had one primary goal: Support the consumer by producing wines not only we were proud to make but wines our consumer friends were proud to purchase."

— Jean Arnold, Co-Owner of Kate Arnold Wines

About the Owners

Kate Arnold was raised to appreciate the outdoors and learned from an early age the importance of animals and agriculture. Jean Arnold developed a passion for wine working for E&J Gallo Wines, managing sales across the Carolinas, Virginias, Sweden, Iceland, Norway, Finland, and Germany — before joining Don Sebastiani & Sons and ultimately founding Kate Arnold Wines.

Kate's marketing expertise and Jean's sales and distribution experience was the flame that ignited Kate Arnold Wines. Today, all three family members work collaboratively, with their daughter Leila also playing a significant role.

Quality & Transparency

We always say we produce wine, not wine product. Wine is a reflection of the vineyard, not of the winemaking. We eschew intrusive techniques and additives — no adjuncts, concentrates, or added sugars find their way into our wines.

Where We Source

We carefully source grapes from exceptional vineyards in California, Oregon, and Washington — selecting the best growing areas for each varietal. Every wine reflects the distinct characteristics of its region, from the expressive Sauvignon Blanc of Lake County to the pure fruit of Oregon's Willamette Valley and the robust Cabernet Sauvignon of Washington's Columbia Valley.
